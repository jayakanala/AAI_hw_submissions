# DevLabs 2.0 — AI Agents Mentorship Program

8-week structured program to go from zero to building real AI agent systems.

Organised by seniors at IIT Bhilai for 1st year students.

---

## Program Overview

| Week | Dates | Topic |
|------|-------|-------|
| Week 1 | May 20 – Jun 1 | What is an AI Agent? |
| Week 2 | Jun 2 – Jun 8 | Tool Use & Function Calling |
| Week 3 | Jun 9 – Jun 15 | Visual Workflows with n8n |
| Week 4 | Jun 16 – Jun 22 | LangGraph – Stateful Agents |
| Week 5 | Jun 23 – Jun 29 | Multi-Agent Systems |
| Week 6 | Jun 30 – Jul 6 | RAG Intro + MCP |
| Week 7 | Jul 7 – Jul 13 | AI Safety & Jailbreaking |
| Week 8 | Jul 14 – Jul 20 | Capstone Project |

---

## How to Submit

1. Fork this repository
2. Navigate to the relevant week's `submissions/` folder
3. Create a folder with your name: `submissions/your-name/`
4. Add your work (code, notes, screenshots)
5. Open a Pull Request
