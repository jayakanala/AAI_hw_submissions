#BOOK STORE AI AGENT
##Domain Chosen
##Book store Assistant
This agent help users:
. Search for books
. Check book prices
. Check book Availability
. Recent conversation history

This agent uses:
. Hugging Face interfence
. python
. short term memory

##Features
. Book Search
. Price of book
. Check Availability
. Conversation history

##Sample Outputs
EXAMPLE 1
You: Suggest me some python books
Agent: Based on your interest in Python, here are a few great books:
1. **Python Crash Course** by Eric Matthes - Perfect for beginners, this book covers the basics and gets you coding quickly.
2. **Automate the Boring Stuff with Python** by Al Sweigart - Learn practical Python skills to automate everyday tasks.
3. **Fluent Python** by Luciano Ramalho - For those looking to deepen their understanding, this book covers advanced topics and best practices.
Which one sounds most appealing to you?

EXAMPLE 2
You: Which one is cheapest?
Agent: To help you save, here are the approximate prices for these books:
1. **Python Crash Course** by Eric Matthes - Usually around $25-$30.
2. **Automate the Boring Stuff with Python** by Al Sweigart - Often around $20-$25.
3. **Fluent Python** by Luciano Ramalho - Typically around $40-$50.
If you're looking for the cheapest option, **"Automate the Boring Stuff with Python"** is usually the most affordable. You can also check online marketplaces like Amazon or used bookstores for better deals.
